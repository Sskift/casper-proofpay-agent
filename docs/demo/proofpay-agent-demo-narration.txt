Meet ProofPay Agent: the evidence-to-payment attestation layer for real-world asset payments on Casper.

The problem is simple. Buyers should not release milestone payments until delivery evidence is trustworthy. Suppliers should not wait through slow manual review. And once AI helps with the decision, every party needs a proof trail they can replay.

ProofPay starts with an operations cockpit. The reviewer sees release readiness, risk, confidence, evidence coverage, and the Casper anchor in one view. The judge walkthrough keeps the route tight: cockpit, trust chain, evidence, Casper proof, and dossier.

Now the evidence enters the system. The Trust Chain accepts a normalized package of invoice, bill of lading, delivery note, temperature log, and supplier registry data. The agent recomputes the decision, evidence hash, decision hash, reasons, next actions, and a mini dossier.

The clean case is ready to release. But this is not a one-path demo. If the invoice amount is wrong, ProofPay moves the milestone to human finance review. If the invoice fingerprint is duplicated, ProofPay blocks release and escalates fraud review.

The Evidence room shows why. Reviewers can inspect documents, extracted claims, timeline events, policy reasons, and follow-up actions. The AI is bounded: it recommends approve, hold, or reject, but humans still control the real payment action.

Casper is the trust anchor. The dashboard exposes Testnet transaction hashes, block heights, named keys, stored URefs, evidence hashes, decision hashes, and replay commands. This prototype does not custody funds. It records the payment decision proof.

Now for the real case. We took a fresh cold-chain delivery on the same trade lane, with a new shipment id, invoice, evidence fingerprints, and amount. ProofPay assessed the case as approve, generated a new evidence hash and decision hash, and the operator signed locally.

That fresh case is now recorded on Casper Testnet as transaction d285146cbf4db68b63ae20ca5c8b9d3e86f6626f254e54f71512553723c8a2ca. The hosted API returns verified, and the stored URef contains the same milestone id, hashes, decision, confidence, and risk score.

That same proof is packaged in the Audit Dossier for buyers, suppliers, arbiters, and judges.

ProofPay does the evidence work. Humans control payment release. Casper makes the decision auditable.
