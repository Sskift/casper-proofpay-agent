Meet ProofPay Agent: an agentic milestone escrow cockpit for real-world asset payments on Casper. Before money moves, the agent checks whether delivery evidence is actually trustworthy.

The cockpit gives reviewers the whole decision in one glance: readiness, risk, confidence, evidence coverage, and the Casper anchor. The left rail keeps the active room visible, so the workflow feels like an operating system, not a slide deck.

Now the signal room brings the proof to life. Risk drops as evidence is reviewed. Escrow movement is visible. Cold-chain telemetry stays inside contract bounds. Coverage shows every document that passed.

This is the real advantage: the Trust Chain. External evidence comes in as a validated package. The agent makes a bounded payment recommendation. Then the settlement runbook tells the supplier, buyer, arbiter, and Casper exactly what happens next.

The Evidence room keeps the AI explainable. Reviewers can move through documents, normalized claims, timeline events, reasons, and follow-up actions. No black box. Every decision leaves a trace.

Casper is the trust anchor. The dashboard exposes the Testnet transaction, block height, named key, stored URef, public key, payload, and the deploy command used to reproduce the attestation.

The Audit Dossier packages the proof for judges and operators: policy trace, evidence hash, decision hash, local transaction, Casper transaction, verifier checks, and a copy-ready JSON record.

And the same system handles exceptions. If the invoice amount is too high, ProofPay moves to human finance review. If a duplicate invoice appears, it blocks release and escalates fraud review.

The value is not just a dashboard. ProofPay links off-chain evidence, agentic review, human release control, and Casper attestation into one auditable payment workflow.
